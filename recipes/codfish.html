Codfish

Description

Traditional portuguese recipe

Grill codfish

Ingredients

  • 1 Kg of codfish in fillets
  • 1,5 Kg of potatoes
  • 2 big onions
  • 5 cloves garlick
  • olive oil
  • salt

Steps

  1. Make a fire on your grill
  2. When the embers are readdy, rinse and dry the codfish.
  3. Lightly oil the grates and place the fillets in the grill.
  4. Grill the fillets until they achieve a golden/browned color on both sides.
